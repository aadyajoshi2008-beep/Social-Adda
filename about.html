About Social Adda

Social Adda is an initiative started by Dr. Supriya J, which provides a social platform to kids with health conditions and impairments.

We aim to meet once a month, without parents, explore new places, make friends, and have some fun, so that they too can have a sense of belonging to the community.

Hence, our motto is, Inclusion and Beyond.

Some of our activities were-

  • Trampoline park
  • Movie Time [Pavillion mall]
  • Pottery session
  • Sports Day
  • Drum Circle
